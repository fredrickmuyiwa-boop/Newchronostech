Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

ChronosTech ("ChronosTech," "we," "us," or "our") operates a global recruitment and talent acquisition platform connecting employers with skilled professionals. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, whether you're an employer sourcing talent or a professional exploring opportunities through our site.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the site.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in a few different ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, phone number, resume/CV, work history, or company details, when you fill out a form, submit an application, or contact us.
  • Information collected automatically — including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referral source, gathered through cookies and similar tracking technologies.
  • Information from third parties — such as data provided by advertising or analytics partners that helps us understand site traffic and ad performance.

2. How we use your information

  • To connect employers with candidates and facilitate recruitment services
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website, content, and user experience
  • To serve relevant advertising, including through Google AdSense (see Section 5)
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site functional, understand how visitors use it, and support advertising. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work as intended if you do.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Employers or candidates, as necessary to facilitate a recruitment match
  • Service providers who help us operate the site (hosting, analytics, email delivery)
  • Advertising partners, such as Google, in connection with ads shown on our site
  • Authorities, where required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Advertising and Google AdSense

Our site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies by visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

UK/EEA residents have these rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR. US residents in certain states may have additional rights under applicable state privacy laws. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. International data transfers

As a global recruitment platform, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and United Kingdom. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for professional use by individuals who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, with the "last updated" date revised accordingly.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at privacy@chronostech.space.
